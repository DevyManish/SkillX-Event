What is SkillX?

SkillX is a dynamic and engaging event that showcases the ideas and models of engineering students, focusing on new technology and innovation.

It provides a platform for students to demonstrate their skills and offers a unique learning experience for attendees.

Who can attend SkillX?

SkillX is open to school students who are eager to learn about emerging technologies and witness the creative innovations of engineering students.

The event encourages curiosity and participation from all who have an interest in the technological advancements shaping our world..

What are the benefits of attending SkillX?

Attending SkillX allows school students to learn about cutting-edge technologies and innovative concepts directly from engineering students.

It serves as a source of inspiration, nurturing a passion for innovation and providing a glimpse into the future of technology. Additionally, it offers a networking opportunity for students to connect with like-minded individuals and potential mentors in the field of technology and engineering.

How can I participate in SkillX?

If you're an engineering student interested in showcasing your ideas and models at SkillX, you can contact the event organizers for more information on registration and participation details.

School students can attend the event by registering or coordinating with their educational institutions..

What can I expect at SkillX?

At SkillX, you can expect to be inspired by groundbreaking ideas, models, and projects. Through interactive displays, presentations, and hands-on demonstrations, you'll have the opportunity to explore new technologies like cloud, web, and Android development.

Engaging with passionate engineering students, you'll gain insights into their expertise and the limitless possibilities of the technological world.
